Solmaz Zeidi

Solmaz Zeidi BASc, BAS (Hons) Architect, Associate

Qualifications
RIBA
MA – Sheffield University
Dip Arch – Sheffield University
Academic Appointments
2009–ongoing: Member if the Essex Design Initiative, Design Review Panel
Consultancy Appointments
2009–ongoing: Member of the Essex Design Initiative Review Panel

Solmaz joined Proctor and Matthews early in 2012 with a range of experience working on educational, commercial and leisure projects, including various universities, student centres and laboratory facilities internationally.

Since joining the team she has been involved in a wide range of projects at the concept and planning stages and has a particular interest in tradition, immutable principles of origin, Islamic and Persian architecture and their collective influence on historical living patterns in Middle Eastern residential dwellings.

Her recent involvement includes Social Housing projects and a mixed use development in Mumbai, Bexhill Masterplan and Business Centre, and CB1; a major new residential and commercial development in central Cambridge with Skanska.

She was promoted to Associate in 2013.
